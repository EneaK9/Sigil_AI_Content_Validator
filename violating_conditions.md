# Content Violation Conditions

This document outlines all conditions that will cause a post to be flagged for violating platform Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

---

## 1. Violence and Threats

A post will be flagged if it contains:

- **Credible threats of violence** against individuals or groups
- **Calls for violence** against people or organizations
- **Incitement to violence** or encouragement of violent acts
- **Celebration/glorification of violence** or violent events
- **Instructions for making weapons** or causing harm
- **Statements of intent to commit violence**
- **Threats that could lead to death or serious injury**

**Severity:** HIGH

---

## 2. Hate Speech

A post will be flagged if it contains attacks based on protected characteristics:

### Protected Characteristics:
- Race or ethnicity
- National origin
- Religious affiliation
- Sexual orientation
- Sex, gender, or gender identity
- Caste
- Serious disease or disability

### Types of Hate Speech:
- **Tier 1 (Most Severe):**
  - Dehumanizing comparisons to animals, insects, pathogens, or subhuman beings
  - Mocking hate crime victims
  - Calls for violence, death, or serious harm against protected groups

- **Tier 2:**
  - Generalizations stating inferiority of a group
  - Expressions of contempt or disgust toward protected groups
  - Cursing at a group based on protected characteristics

- **Tier 3:**
  - Calls for segregation or exclusion
  - Slurs used as insults
  - Hateful stereotypes

**Severity:** HIGH (Tier 1), MEDIUM-HIGH (Tier 2), MEDIUM (Tier 3)

---

## 3. Bullying and Harassment

A post will be flagged if it contains:

- **Targeted harassment campaigns** against individuals
- **Repeated unwanted contact** with someone who has expressed they don't want it
- **Attacks on private individuals** with degrading content
- **Sexual harassment** of any kind
- **Attacks on minors**
- **Sharing private information to harass** (doxxing)
- **Threats to release intimate images**
- **Coordinated harassment** or brigading

**Severity:** HIGH

---

## 4. Adult Sexual Content and Exploitation

A post will be flagged if it contains:

- **Non-consensual intimate imagery** (revenge porn)
- **Threats to share intimate images** without consent
- **Sexual services offers** or solicitation
- **Sexual exploitation** of adults
- **Nudity** (platform-dependent, some artistic exceptions)
- **Sexual intercourse depictions**
- **Partially nude or sexually suggestive images** (platform-dependent)

**Severity:** HIGH (non-consensual), MEDIUM (consensual but against policy)

---

## 5. Child Safety Violations

A post will be flagged if it contains:

- **Sexual content involving minors** (any age under 18)
- **Suggestive content involving minors**
- **Abusive content involving minors**
- **Predatory or inappropriate behavior** toward minors
- **Child exploitation material** of any kind

**Severity:** CRITICAL (immediate removal, potential legal action)

---

## 6. Suicide and Self-Harm

A post will be flagged if it:

- **Promotes or encourages suicide** or self-injury
- **Shares instructions** for methods of suicide or self-harm
- **Glorifies or celebrates** suicide or self-injury
- **Challenges or dares** that could lead to self-harm

**Severity:** HIGH

---

## 7. Dangerous Organizations and Individuals

A post will be flagged if it:

- **Promotes terrorist organizations**
- **Supports or praises hate groups**
- **Glorifies criminal organizations**
- **Celebrates mass murderers or serial killers**
- **Recruits for dangerous organizations**
- **Shares propaganda** for dangerous organizations

**Severity:** HIGH

---

## 8. Privacy Violations

A post will be flagged if it:

- **Shares personal information without consent** (doxxing)
- **Publishes private addresses** (home, work)
- **Shares financial information** (bank accounts, credit cards)
- **Shares medical information** without consent
- **Reveals confidential information**
- **Shares phone numbers or email addresses** to harass

**Severity:** HIGH (with intent to harass), MEDIUM (without malicious intent)

---

## 9. Spam and Inauthentic Behavior

A post will be flagged if it:

- **Is from a fake account**
- **Is part of coordinated inauthentic behavior**
- **Is spam** (repetitive, unsolicited commercial content)
- **Uses artificially amplified engagement** (fake likes, follows, comments)
- **Is misleading content designed to deceive**
- **Contains repetitive comments or content**
- **Contacts people for commercial purposes without consent**

**Severity:** MEDIUM-HIGH

---

## 10. Misinformation

A post will be flagged if it:

- **Spreads false news** that could cause harm
- **Contains health misinformation** (especially regarding vaccines, treatments)
- **Uses manipulated media** (deepfakes, doctored images)
- **Could cause physical harm** due to false information
- **Contains election misinformation** (voter fraud claims, voting process lies)

**Severity:** HIGH (potential physical harm), MEDIUM (general misinformation)

---

## 11. Intellectual Property Violations

A post will be flagged if it:

- **Infringes copyrights** (using content without permission)
- **Infringes trademarks** (unauthorized use of brands)
- **Promotes counterfeit goods**
- **Shares copyrighted music, videos, or images** without permission

**Severity:** MEDIUM

---

## 12. Illegal Activities

A post will be flagged if it:

- **Facilitates or organizes criminal activity**
- **Sells regulated goods illegally** (firearms, alcohol, tobacco, drugs)
- **Sells prescription medications** without authorization
- **Solicits or facilitates illegal transactions**
- **Promotes illegal services**

**Severity:** HIGH

---

## 13. Account Integrity Violations

A post/account will be flagged if it:

- **Uses a fake identity** (not real name where required)
- **Impersonates another person or entity**
- **Is a purchased or sold account**
- **Is a shared account** (violates TOS on some platforms)
- **Engages in ban evasion**

**Severity:** MEDIUM-HIGH

---

## 14. Bot/Automation Indicators (Enhanced Flag)

A post from an account will receive enhanced scrutiny if:

- **Account shows bot-like behavior** (see bot detection signals)
- **Account has high posting frequency** (>5 posts/day average)
- **Account has extreme recent activity** (>15 posts/day in last 30 days)
- **Account has excessive commenting** (>20 comments/day)
- **New account with high activity** (<30 days old with >50 posts)
- **Account shows coordinated behavior** with other suspected bots

**Note:** Bot status does not change whether content violates policy, but BOT accounts spreading violating content are flagged as higher risk due to amplification potential.

**Severity:** Modifier (increases severity of other violations)

---

## Severity Levels

| Level | Description | Typical Action |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **CRITICAL** | Immediate danger, illegal content | Immediate removal, potential legal referral |
| **HIGH** | Clear policy violation | Content removal, possible account action |
| **MEDIUM** | Policy violation, less severe | Content removal or warning |
| **LOW** | Borderline violation | Warning, reduced distribution |

---

## Platform-Specific Notes

### Facebook
- Requires real identity
- Stricter on misinformation
- Multi-tier hate speech classification

### Instagram
- Stricter on nudity/sexual content
- Focus on authentic engagement
- Strong intellectual property enforcement

### Reddit
- Community rules vary by subreddit
- Focus on authentic participation
- Strong privacy protection emphasis

### X (Twitter)
- More permissive on controversial speech
- Focus on threats and harassment
- Strong bot/spam detection

### TikTok
- Strict on content involving minors
- Focus on dangerous challenges
- Strong copyright enforcement on music

---

## How Violations Are Detected

1. **Text Analysis** - Post text is analyzed against policy keywords and patterns
2. **Image Analysis** - Images are scanned for violating visual content
3. **Video Transcript Analysis** - Audio from videos is transcribed and analyzed
4. **Account Analysis** - Bot detection signals are evaluated
5. **Context Analysis** - Platform-specific policies are applied
6. **AI Judgment** - Claude AI makes final determination with confidence score

---

## Output Format

When a violation is detected, the system returns:

```json
{
  "verdict": "FAIL",
  "violations": [
    {
      "rule": "Exact rule name from policy",
      "severity": "HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW",
      "explanation": "Why this is a violation",
      "policy_reference": "Section from policy document",
      "quote": "Exact phrase or description that triggered violation"
    }
  ],
  "confidence": 0.0 - 1.0,
  "recommendation": "What should be changed or removed"
}
```
