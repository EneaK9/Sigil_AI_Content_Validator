# LinkedIn Terms of Service - Key Policy Points

## User Agreement Overview

By using LinkedIn, members agree to:
- Maintain accurate profile information
- Use real identity and professional credentials
- Not misrepresent qualifications or affiliations
- Comply with all applicable laws
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Not engage in unauthorized data collection

## Account Requirements

### Identity
- Must use real name
- Must provide accurate professional information
- May only have one personal account
- Business pages have separate requirements

### Age Requirements
- Must be at least 16 years old (or minimum age in your country)
- Must have capacity to enter into a binding agreement

## Content Ownership and Rights

### Your Content
- You retain ownership of content you post
- You grant LinkedIn a license to use, display, and distribute your content
- This license is non-exclusive and worldwide
- License ends when you delete content (unless shared by others)

### LinkedIn's Rights
- LinkedIn owns the platform, features, and services
- LinkedIn's intellectual property is protected
- Members may not copy, modify, or reverse engineer LinkedIn's services

## Prohibited Activities

### Data and Security
- No unauthorized scraping or data collection
- No circumventing security measures
- No accessing accounts you don't own
- No sharing login credentials
- No creating false or misleading accounts

### Commercial Restrictions
- No unauthorized commercial use
- No selling access to LinkedIn features
- No using LinkedIn for illegal pyramid schemes
- No unauthorized advertising or solicitation

### Malicious Activities
- No distributing malware or viruses
- No phishing or credential harvesting
- No denial of service attacks
- No interference with platform operations

## Termination

LinkedIn may:
- Remove content that violates policies
- Suspend or terminate accounts
- Restrict access to features
- Take legal action for serious violations

Members may:
- Delete their account at any time
- Request data export before deletion
- Appeal account restrictions

## Dispute Resolution

- Most disputes resolved through arbitration
- Class action waiver applies
- Some disputes may go to court
- LinkedIn's liability is limited per agreement

## Updates to Terms

- LinkedIn may update terms with notice
- Continued use constitutes acceptance
- Material changes communicated via email or platform notifications

## Privacy

- Data collection and use governed by Privacy Policy
- Members control certain privacy settings
- LinkedIn shares data with affiliates and service providers
- Data may be transferred internationally

## Professional Standards

LinkedIn is a professional network. Members are expected to:
- Maintain professional conduct
- Engage constructively in discussions
- Respect diverse viewpoints
- Contribute value to the community
- Report violations when encountered
