# Instagram Community Guidelines

Source: https://help.instagram.com/581066165581870

## Our Commitment to Safety

Instagram is committed to fostering a positive, diverse community. These guidelines help ensure Instagram remains a supportive environment.

## Share Only Photos and Videos You've Taken or Have the Right to Share

- Post content you own or have permission to share
- Don't post content that infringes someone else's intellectual property rights
- Don't share copyrighted music, videos, or images without permission

## Post Photos and Videos That Are Appropriate for a Diverse Audience

We want Instagram to be appropriate for our diverse community. Content that may not be appropriate includes:
- Nudity (some exceptions for artistic purposes)
- Partially nude images
- Close-ups of buttocks
- Sexual intercourse depictions

## Foster Meaningful and Genuine Interactions

Help us stay spam-free by:
- Not artificially collecting likes, followers, or shares
- Not posting repetitive comments or content
- Not contacting people for commercial purposes without consent

## Follow the Law

- Don't use Instagram to facilitate or organize criminal activity
- Don't offer sexual services
- Don't sell regulated goods like firearms, alcohol, or tobacco
- Don't sell prescription medications

## Respect Other Members of the Instagram Community

### We Don't Allow:
- Bullying or harassment
- Hate speech based on race, ethnicity, national origin, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, religious affiliation, disabilities, or diseases
- Threats of violence
- Posting private or confidential information

## Violence and Threats

We remove:
- Credible threats of violence
- Content that encourages violence
- Content that glorifies violence

## Hate Speech

We do not allow:
- Attacks on people based on protected characteristics
- Dehumanizing language
- Calls for exclusion or segregation
- Hateful slurs

## Bullying and Harassment

We do not allow:
- Targeted harassment campaigns
- Repeated unwanted contact
- Sharing private images without consent
- Sexual harassment
- Attacks on private individuals

## Self-Harm and Suicide

We remove content that:
- Promotes or encourages suicide or self-injury
- Provides instructions for self-harm
- Glorifies self-harm

We provide resources for those in distress.

## Dangerous Organizations

We do not allow:
- Terrorist organizations
- Organized hate groups
- Content that praises or supports dangerous organizations

## Intellectual Property

Respect the intellectual property rights of others:
- Don't share content you don't own
- Don't use others' trademarks without permission
- Report violations through our IP reporting tools

## Spam and Fake Accounts

We don't allow:
- Fake accounts
- Accounts that impersonate others
- Spam
- Artificial engagement (fake likes, follows, comments)

## Account Enforcement

Accounts that violate these guidelines may be:
- Warned
- Have content removed
- Have features restricted
- Be suspended
- Be permanently disabled
