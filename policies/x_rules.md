# X (Twitter) Rules and Policies

## Platform Integrity and Authenticity
Policies that promote the health of the public conversation by investigating and mitigating material related to spam, platform manipulation, API abuse, and information operations.

## Safety and Cybercrime
Policies that enforce our principles against abuse, harassment, violence and criminal actions on the X platform.

### Hateful Conduct Policy
You may not directly attack other people based on protected categories.

We are committed to combating abuse motivated by hatred, prejudice or intolerance, particularly abuse that seeks to silence the voices of those who have been historically marginalized. We prohibit behavior that targets individuals or groups with abuse based on their perceived membership in a protected category.

**Protected categories include:**
- Race
- Ethnicity
- National origin
- Caste
- Sexual orientation
- Gender
- Gender identity
- Religious affiliation
- Age
- Disability
- Serious disease

**Prohibited conduct includes:**
- Content that intends to dehumanize, degrade, or reinforce negative stereotypes about a protected category
- Inciting behavior that targets individuals or groups belonging to protected categories
- Content promoting violence, threats, or harassment against protected groups

### Hateful Imagery and Symbols
You may not use hateful images or symbols in your profile image or profile header. You also may not use your username, display name, or profile bio to engage in abusive behavior, such as targeted harassment or expressing hate towards a person, group, or protected category.

### Violence and Threats
- No violent threats against individuals or groups
- No glorification of violence
- No content depicting gratuitous violence

### Harassment and Abuse
- No targeted harassment of individuals
- No coordinated harassment campaigns
- No doxxing or sharing private information
- No non-consensual intimate images

### Sensitive Media
- Adult content must be marked as sensitive
- Gratuitously violent content may be removed
- Content depicting harm to minors is prohibited

## Intellectual Property
Policies that protect Intellectual Property rights of individuals and organizations on the X Platform.

- No copyright infringement
- No trademark violations
- Respect DMCA takedown requests

## Platform Use Guidelines
- No spam or platform manipulation
- No fake accounts or impersonation
- No artificial amplification of content
- No misleading information that could cause harm

## Enforcement Actions
When determining the penalty for violating these policies, we consider:
- Severity of the violation
- Previous record of rule violations
- Context of the content

Enforcement options include:
- Content removal
- Account suspension
- Limiting content visibility
- Requiring content deletion before account access
