# Facebook Community Standards

Source: https://transparency.meta.com/policies/community-standards

## Violence and Incitement

We aim to prevent potential real-world harm that may be related to content on Facebook. We remove content that:
- Contains credible threats of violence against individuals or groups
- Calls for violence against people
- Incites violence or celebrates violence
- Shares instructions for making weapons

### Violent Threats
We do not allow:
- Threats that could lead to death or serious injury
- Threats to specific individuals or groups
- Statements of intent to commit violence

## Hate Speech

We do not allow hate speech on Facebook. Hate speech is defined as:
- Direct attacks against people based on protected characteristics: race, ethnicity, national origin, religious affiliation, sexual orientation, caste, sex, gender, gender identity, serious disease, or disability
- Dehumanizing speech or imagery comparing people to animals, insects, or subhuman beings
- Slurs and stereotypes that degrade or demean protected groups

### Tier 1 (Most Severe)
- Dehumanizing comparisons to animals, pathogens, or subhuman beings
- Mocking hate crime victims
- Calls for segregation or exclusion

### Tier 2
- Generalizations that state inferiority
- Expressions of contempt or disgust
- Cursing at a group based on protected characteristics

### Tier 3
- Calls for segregation
- Slurs used as insults

## Bullying and Harassment

We do not allow:
- Repeated contact with someone who has expressed they don't want it
- Attacks on private individuals
- Sharing private information to harass
- Threats to release intimate images
- Coordinated harassment campaigns

### What We Remove
- Content that targets private individuals with degrading content
- Repeatedly contacting someone against their will
- Sexual harassment of any kind
- Attacks on minors

## Adult Sexual Exploitation

We remove content that:
- Depicts non-consensual intimate imagery
- Shares or threatens to share intimate images without consent
- Offers sexual services
- Facilitates sexual exploitation of adults

## Suicide and Self-Injury

We remove content that:
- Promotes or encourages suicide or self-injury
- Shares instructions for methods of suicide or self-harm
- Glorifies or celebrates suicide or self-injury

We provide resources for people in distress and connect them with support.

## Dangerous Organizations and Individuals

We do not allow:
- Terrorist organizations
- Hate groups
- Criminal organizations
- Mass murderers and serial killers
- Content that praises or supports these entities

## Privacy Violations

We do not allow:
- Sharing personal information without consent
- Doxxing (publishing private info to harass)
- Sharing financial information
- Sharing medical information
- Sharing home addresses

## Spam and Inauthentic Behavior

We do not allow:
- Fake accounts
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior
- Spam
- Artificially amplified content
- Misleading content designed to deceive

## Misinformation

We reduce the spread of:
- False news
- Health misinformation
- Manipulated media
- Content that could cause physical harm
- Election misinformation

## Intellectual Property

We respect intellectual property rights and remove content that:
- Infringes copyrights
- Infringes trademarks
- Contains counterfeit goods

## Account Integrity

Users must:
- Use their real identity
- Not create fake accounts
- Not share accounts
- Not sell or purchase accounts
